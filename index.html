The 20 Most Beautiful Countries In The World

source: https://www.buzzfeed.com/travelguru

South Africa

This setting is on a coastline hailed as the most beautiful sight on earth ever seen. The country has three of the world's ten most beautiful coastal drives including Clarens Drive and Chapman's Peak.



United States

The country has some of the most stunning mountains in the world, including the Colorado Rockies, the Grand Tetons and the Sierra Nevada. Glacier National Park is incredible, and the most awesome forest in the world is Sequoia.

New Zealand

New Zealand's coast is one of the most beautiful on Earth, and Rotorua Geysers are second in the world only to Yellowstone in terms of geothermal activity. Sutherland Falls, is one of the most amazing waterfalls.


China

The world's third largest country has some of its finest scenery. The Mekong River is one of the most extraordinary rivers in the world, arising in the vast Tibetan Plateau. This plateau is the largest and highest plateau in the world.

Namibia

The desert nation of Africa is hauntingly beautiful. It's Germanic influence is evident throughout the country, which is one of the world's natural treasure troves. Etosha national park is indisputably one of the world's greatest game reserves.

Argentina

From the urban splendor of Buenos Aires, rated one of the most beautiful cities in the world, to the massive expanse of the Pampas, Argentina is a land of evocative beauty. Probably the scenic highlight of the country, though, is Patagonia.

Colombia

It is one of the most biodiverse countries in the world, due in part to its incredible altitude differences within the tropics. The country has some of the most impressive sections of the Andes, and Santa Marta is the highest coastal mountain range.

Tanzania

Birthplace of Freddie Mercury, the country is one of the very best wildlife destinations on earth. During the great migrations between Kenya's Maasai Mara and Tanzania's Serengeti, which means 'Endless Plains.


Italy

Probably the most beautiful country in all of Europe, Italy is one of the world's great tourist destinations for a reason. Dolomite Alps are iconic mountains on earth, and the Mediterranean has beautiful landscape on the Amalfi Coast.

Kenya

Land of the Maasai Mara and image of wild Africa. The spectacle of the great migration is one of the greatest in the world, and the views of Kilimanjaro from Amboseli National Park are ironically probably better than from Tanzania itself.

Nepal

The mountain country of Nepal is predictably stunning. The capital of Kathmandu is possibly the most famous, and has exceptionally distinctive architecture, but the second largest town of Pokhara is the real jaw-dropper.

Canada

The Canadian Rockies are justifiably famous as amongst the most beautiful in the world. The view of the Twelve Apostles from Moraine Lake in Banff National Park is one of the most beautiful park, Vancouver a lovely city.


Switzerland

Europe's mountain nation encompasses probably the very most beautiful parts of the Alps. The Matterhorn is listed two on the most incredible mountains in the world, and the Bernese Oberland one of the most beautiful highlands.

Uganda

"The Pearl of Africa" is incredible. Though a small country, it is the starting point of one of the most amazing rivers in the world – and its very longest - the Nile. The river is born in the 'mountains of the moon', known as the Rwenzoris.

India

Home to stunning places like Rajasthan and Goa, India has everything from tropical beaches to the glacier capped Himalayas. From the town of Varanasi, to the great monument of love which is the Taj Mahal.

Chile

Chile spans almost from the equator to the sub Antarctic, making it the longest contiguous country in the world as measured by latitude. Crammed into the almost 40 latitude degrees are some of the world's most beautiful places.

Brazil

The world's most biodiverse country is also its fifth largest. Within such a massive area, it is unsurprising that there are scenic highlights. Foremost is undoubtedly the unparalleled
Amazon Rainforest and Amazon River.

Norway

Land of trolls and fjords, Norway is one of the most beautiful countries in Europe. While fjords such as Geiranger, Nærøyfjord and Sognefjord dominate the mental images of this Scandinavian gem.


Philippines

the Philippines is one of the most stunning countries in the world. It's made up of over 7000 stunning islands in the Pacific Ocean! The two largest islands in the chain, Luzon and Mindanao. Home to the immortal Cardo Dalisay.

Australia

Australia has 16,000 miles of coastline, boasting some of the most beautiful beaches in the world. The Great Barrier Reef is one of the world's natural wonders, the Island of Tasmania is beautiful, and Sydney is a widely renowned city.
